Privacy Policy

PM Smart · Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the PM Smart mobile application ("the App", "we", "our") handles user information. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about data practices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

PM Smart is a client application that connects directly to a smart home server you own and control (e.g. Home Assistant). The App does not:

  • Collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers
  • Track your usage, location, or device behavior
  • Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Share any data with external parties
  • Require an account or user registration

We do not operate any backend server that receives your data. All communication happens directly between your device and your own smart home server.

2. Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data only on your device:

  • Server connection details: LAN/WAN URLs you enter
  • Access token: stored securely in iOS Keychain, encrypted by the operating system
  • App preferences: room layouts, device names, language, theme, and similar settings
  • UI state: which devices appear on Home, favorite order, etc.

This data never leaves your device. When you delete the App, all locally stored data is removed by iOS.

3. Network Permissions

The App requests the following iOS permissions:

  • Local Network access: required to discover and connect to your smart home server on your home Wi-Fi (LAN).
  • Camera access: only used when you tap "Scan QR" to quickly enter an access token. The camera is not used at any other time. No images are stored or transmitted.
  • Photo Library access: only used when you choose "From photo" to import a QR code image. The selected image is processed locally to decode the QR code. The image is not uploaded or stored elsewhere.

All permissions are optional. The App will function normally if you decline, though some features (auto-discovery, QR scanning) will be unavailable.

4. Communication With Your Smart Home Server

When you enter a server address and access token, the App communicates directly with that server using standard HTTPS or HTTP (depending on your configuration). We have no visibility into or control over what data your smart home server processes or stores. Please refer to the privacy policy of your server provider (e.g. Home Assistant Privacy Policy) for details on how that platform handles your data.

5. Demo Mode

If you use the "Try Demo" feature, the App operates entirely with sample data generated on your device. No network requests are made, and no data is sent anywhere.

6. Children's Privacy

PM Smart is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.
